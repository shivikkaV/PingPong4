AI Ping Pong Game







Instructions

  1. Use your nose to move the pad!

  2. Move your nose up to move the pad up!
  3. Move your nose down to move the pad down!

  4. Each time you hit the ball will go faster and faster!

  5. If you miss, the ball's speed will go back to 0 and you will lose a life
  6. If you lose all 3 lives, the game is over
